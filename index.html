Email list cleanser

Before you upload contacts to Marketing Cloud Account Engagement (MCAE), you need to know your data is clean. Invalid emails break automations. Field length violations cause import failures. Duplicate records waste budget.

This tool validates everything before import—email format, field limits, data quality, duplicates, and more—and processes everything right in your browser.

Best of all, your contact data never leaves your computer. Fix issues with inline editing, apply bulk corrections, and export clean lists ready for MCAE.
